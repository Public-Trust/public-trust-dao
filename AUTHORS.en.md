# Authors and Stewards — Public Trust DAO

## Project author and initiator

**Fedor Grigoriev** (Федор Григорьев) — author of the idea and initiator of Public Trust DAO.

**Project contact:** fedorgrigorev@proton.me (official public address, temporary —
until mail on the project's own domain is set up).

> **Authorship ≠ ownership.** Under the project's constitution (see
> [`docs/en/CONSTITUTION.md`](docs/en/CONSTITUTION.md), articles 1–3 and §6.2),
> **no one owns the project**. "Author and initiator" recognizes the origin of
> the idea — it is not a property right and not power over funds. The founder
> takes part as an **ordinary participant/steward** (one vote, like every other
> verified human — see [`docs/en/GOVERNANCE.md`](docs/en/GOVERNANCE.md)).
> The project belongs to its **mission, constitution, and community**.

## Stewards of the mission

Stewards are people (and supporting AI modules) temporarily serving the mission.
Neither a human nor an AI controls funds single-handedly. The list of multisig
steward-operators will appear after Stage 4 (Governance) and will be published
openly — addresses are created and confirmed by the community/operator, not by
the agent.

- **Author/initiator:** Fedor Grigoriev — participant-steward (1 vote).
- **AI builder agent:** an autonomous module that runs the public build inside
  the constitution's guardrails. It **holds no funds and has no power**
  (see [`BUILDER.md`](BUILDER.md), [`docs/en/PRINCIPLES.md`](docs/en/PRINCIPLES.md)).

## Contributing

The project is open. All contributions are made publicly (code — AGPL-3.0, docs —
CC-BY-SA-4.0; see [`LICENSE`](LICENSE) and [`LICENSE-DOCS`](LICENSE-DOCS)).
Authorship is preserved via git history and the decisions registry
[`governance/registry/`](governance/registry/). Contributing grants no power over
the treasury — distribution is governed by community voting and the constitution.
