## 0. Message rails (read BEFORE any publication)

Any public text about the project must comply with the
[constitutional prohibitions](PRINCIPLES.md). This is not a "marketing tone" — it
is the project's law. Literally forbidden:

- ❌ promising returns, profit, "earnings", ROI, "x's", passive income;
- ❌ calling participation an "investment", "deposit at interest", "a token that
  will go up";
- ❌ building a pyramid; paying for recruiting people; referral bonuses;
- ❌ pressuring with urgency/scarcity ("buy now", "spots running out");
- ❌ guaranteeing anyone will receive help in exchange for money;
- ❌ hiding that everything is open and verifiable, or overstating readiness
  (we are at bootstrap/testnet — we say so honestly).

✅ What we say instead: this is a **public good** and **mutual aid**; the treasury
is **shared and transparent**; **no one owns it**; help follows the
[priority of need](PRIORITIES.md), not payment; everything is **open and
verifiable**; the project is at **bootstrap** — built in public.

> The principle in one line: **we invite people to help and to receive help — not
> to invest and earn.**

**How it works (3 steps):**

1. **Request.** A person in a hard situation describes their need (housing, food,
   healthcare, …). Personal data is not published — only an anonymized case ID.
2. **Community review.** The decision is made not by one person and not by an AI
   alone, but by guardians following the constitution and the
   [priority of need](PRIORITIES.md).
3. **Targeted help.** Where possible we pay the need directly (rent → to the
   landlord, treatment → to the clinic) rather than "cash in hand" — so help
   reaches its purpose. See the
   [targeted-disbursement model](ESCROW-TARGETED-DISBURSEMENT.md).

**What we help with:**
housing and rent · food · treatment and medicine · families and children · the
elderly · education and reskilling · employment · emergencies · animal welfare ·
social integration.

**Why this can be trusted:**

- 🔓 **Everything is open** — code, documents, decisions. Anyone can check.
- 👥 **No owner** — the treasury is run by several guardians (3 signatures of 5);
  the founder is an ordinary participant.
- 🧾 **Verifiable** — every decision is in a public registry that cannot be
  rewritten after the fact (tamper-evident).
- ⚖️ **Fair** — help follows the priority of real need, not payment or "who you
  know".

**Get help:** *(soon)* request submission will appear in the web dApp — see the

in public.

**How to help:** you can help through action (volunteering, development,
translation, legal/medical expertise) and, over time, by contributing to the
shared treasury (only after an audit and launch, with no promise of returns).

**Footer:** ⚠️ Public Trust DAO is a public good, NOT an investment and NOT a
financial pyramid. Open source: github.com/Public-Trust/public-trust-dao ·

## 5. FAQ — "this is a public good, NOT an investment"

**Is this a way to earn / invest?**
No. We explicitly forbid ourselves from promising returns, guaranteeing profit, or
calling participation an investment. It is a mutual-aid fund, not a financial
product.

**Then is it a pyramid / MLM?**
No. In a pyramid people are paid for recruiting others. With us this is
[forbidden by the constitution](PRINCIPLES.md) literally: reward never depends on
recruiting participants, and there are no referral bonuses.

**Is there a token that will go up?**
We do not sell a token as a way to earn and we promise no price growth. Any
technical tokens (if they appear) serve governance and accounting, not
speculation.

**Who owns everything and controls the money?**
No one alone. The treasury is shared, under multisig (3 signatures of 5
guardians). The founder is an ordinary participant with the same rights. Neither a
human nor an AI owns the funds.

**How do I know the money won't be stolen?**
Everything is public and verifiable: open code, open documents, a tamper-evident
public decision registry (any rewriting of history shows immediately). Where
possible, help is paid directly to the provider of the need.

**Who decides who gets help?**
Not one person and not an AI alone, but guardians following the constitution and
the [priority of need](PRIORITIES.md): threat to life → housing → treatment →
food → children → elderly → and onward. AI agents only help enforce the rules;
they hold no power.

**Are you collecting money right now?**
No. The project is at bootstrap stage. A real treasury with real funds is possible
only after an independent audit of the contracts and with a live guardian multisig.
For now everything is on testnet and in the form of open specifications.

**How can I help if not with money?**
Through action: volunteering, development, translating documentation (RU/EN), legal
and medical expertise, spreading honest information about the project.

**Where can I verify all this?**
The open repository: github.com/Public-Trust/public-trust-dao — manifesto,
constitution, decision registry and all the code are there.
