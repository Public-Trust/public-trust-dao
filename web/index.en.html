An open system of mutual aid and public goods

Public Trust DAO provides people with basic safety, human dignity, housing, food, medical care, education and a path back to self-reliance — and the chance, once helped, to help others in turn.

How to participate Code & documents
This is NOT an investment, NOT a financial pyramid, NOT a profit-making instrument. The project promises no yield and guarantees no profit. It is open mutual-aid infrastructure — a public good governed by a constitution, a transparent on-chain treasury, open source code and the community.
Why we exist

Mission

To build an open, decentralized system of mutual support that gives people a dignified life and the ability to help others.

No owner

No person, organization, state or AI controls the funds alone. The founder is an ordinary participant. The project belongs to its mission, its constitution and the community.

Everything is open

All documents are open, every decision is explained, every operation is published, every change is recorded — as immutably as is technically possible.

AI serves, never rules

AI agents are service modules that help uphold the constitution. The AI holds no power and owns no funds.

The law of the project

Principles and prohibitions

The prohibitions below are mandatory and literal — in the code, in the AI agents, in governance and in any public text.

  • No promising of yield
  • No guaranteeing of profit
  • No financial pyramids
  • No paying for recruiting people
  • No concentration of power
  • No hiding of operations
  • No using the fund for personal gain
  • No corruption
  • No favoritism
  • No discrimination

Every person has dignity regardless of income, origin, education, age, nationality, state of health or social standing.

Fairness when funds are limited

Priority of fair distribution

When funds are limited, requests with higher priority are considered first. Priority does not waive abuse checks and does not depend on a person's origin or status — only on the nature of the need.

The real-funds latch

Real money enters the contracts only after an independent audit, a separate community approval, and moving the treasury under a 3-of-5 Safe multisig of living people. Until then — test networks only.
